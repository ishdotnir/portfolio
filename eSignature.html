Case study

eSignature

Imagine: You're an employer who wants their employee to sign several documents at once. You add all the relevant documents to 1 folder and add the employee as a signee. They log in and sign the required documents in record time. The eSignature tool provides a platform-level capability for users to sign documents quickly and efficiently.

  • Role: Content Designer, UX Strategist
  • Company: Intuit
  • Timeline: Feb 2024 - March 2024
  • Team: 1 PM · 1 PD · 1 Researcher

Process

  1. Research
  2. Content & IA
  3. Design
  4. Validation

Research

Interviews, support ticket review, funnel analysis.

Content & IA

Information architecture, taxonomy, naming, error copy.

Design

Wireframes → hi-fi screens, states, and flows.

Validation

Usability testing, copy tests, shipping with metrics.

Outcomes

  • +17% activation within 28 days of launch.
  • −32% payment-related tickets in Q2.
  • Shipped a content pattern library for error states and empty states.
