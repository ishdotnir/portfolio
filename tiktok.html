TikTok Minis

We want users to enjoy the app and have reasons to stay. Mini apps and mini games turn casual visits into daily habits, giving them a lightweight, non-taxing reason to engage more with us, and have a good time.

TikTok for Developers

On the flip side, there's a developer who wants to launch their mini app or mini game on TikTok. Another wants to add a 'Share to TikTok' button in their app. We're making sure they can tap into TikTok's technology and APIs in the most convenient way possible way. Welcome to TikTok for Developers.

Account Security

Getting locked out of your TikTok account? Not on our watch. If it happens, we'll guide you back in with recovery options that are easy, safe, and made to keep you in control.
